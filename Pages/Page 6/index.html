Character Development and Interactions

The series is set in the year 2071 and follows the adventures of a ragtag group of bounty hunters (known as "cowboys") traveling through space aboard the spaceship Bebop. Each episode typically focuses on a standalone story, but overarching plotlines and character backstories are interwoven throughout the series.
